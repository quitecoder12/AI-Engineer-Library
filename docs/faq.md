# Frequently Asked Questions

## Do I need programming experience?

Basic programming knowledge is recommended, but every topic is explained from first principles.

---

## Why learn Python?

Python is the dominant programming language for AI, machine learning, and data science due to its readability and extensive ecosystem.

---

## Is this library free?

Yes. The goal is to make high-quality AI engineering education freely available.

---

## Do I need a powerful computer?

No. Most examples can be run on a standard laptop. Cloud services and local LLM options will be covered where appropriate.

---

## Which operating system should I use?

Windows, macOS, and Linux are all supported. Commands will be provided for each platform whenever necessary.

---

## How often is the library updated?

The library is intended to evolve alongside Python and the AI ecosystem, with new topics and revisions added over time.

---

## Can I contribute?

Yes. Contributions, corrections, and improvements are welcome once contribution guidelines are published.
